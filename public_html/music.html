Keeping Things Dead Simple.

In 2019, I took a chance on my hobby of guitar playing and joined the four-piece classic rock band Dead Simple as a rhythm guitarist. Dead Simple was started by the bassist, Paul, in Nanticoke, PA in 2008. Paul assembled the current version of the band after moving to Elysburg, PA. During my tenure with Dead Simple, I have transitioned from playing rhythm guitar to performing lead vocals and lead guitar. A link to our Facebook page can be found here: Dead Simple's Facebook Page.

Dead Simple's current Lineup: Leo, Mike, Val, and Paul (left to right).
